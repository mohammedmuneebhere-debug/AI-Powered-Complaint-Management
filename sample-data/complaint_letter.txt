COMPLAINT TYPE: Packaging Defect

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
During warehouse receiving inspection on March 7, 2024, we discovered that 12 out of 48 shipping cartons from batch AMX-2023-1156 have compromised primary packaging. Specifically, the blister packs show signs of moisture ingress with visible condensation inside the foil seals. Three cartons had torn secondary packaging, exposing the blister packs to ambient humidity during transit.

The affected cartons were part of shipment PO-88421 received on March 5, 2024. We have isolated all affected inventory in our quarantine area (Zone Q-14).

INITIAL ASSESSMENT:
- Initial Severity: High
- Priority: Urgent
- Potential impact on product stability and efficacy

We request batch recall assessment and replacement shipment at your earliest convenience.
